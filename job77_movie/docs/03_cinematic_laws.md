# Job 77 – Cinematic Laws (Simplified)

These laws ensure every movie has professional quality and consistency.

## Core Laws

### 1. Continuity Law
- Characters must look the same across scenes
- Environment and lighting must stay consistent
- Scale and perspective must match between shots

### 2. Camera Intent Law
Every camera movement must have a clear purpose:
- Dolly in = focus / importance
- Pan = reveal or follow action
- Static = stability or tension

### 3. Subject Tracking Law
Main subject should stay properly framed unless intentionally breaking the rule for effect.

### 4. Motion Law
Movement should feel natural. Avoid random or jittery camera work.

### 5. PDF-to-Movie Canon Law
The original PDF/document is the source of truth. All visuals and dialogue must stay faithful to the source material.

### 6. Quality Gate Law
No movie is delivered until it passes the Governor check.

## How These Laws Are Used
- Applied during Storyboard stage
- Checked again during Rendering
- Final validation done by the Governor before delivery

# Job 77 – Governor (Quality Validation System)

## Purpose
The Governor is the final quality gate. Nothing gets delivered to the customer until it passes.

## What the Governor Checks

- **Continuity** — Characters, environment, and style stay consistent
- **Cinematic Laws** — Camera moves and motion follow the rules
- **Faithfulness to Source** — Movie matches the original PDF/document
- **Technical Quality** — No major rendering errors, audio sync issues, or broken scenes
- **Length & Structure** — Movie matches the requested format (short, mid, or feature)

## Governor Workflow
## Current Status
For now, the Governor can be a simple function that checks basic rules.  
Later it can become a full agent that uses AI to review quality.

## Example Rules (Simple Version)
- Video must be longer than 30 seconds
- No major black screens or frozen frames
- Audio must be present
- Text overlays must be readable
